What is BOOST?

BOOST is a new product by LEGO(R) which is targeted towards young children. Like MINDSTORMS, BOOST is a programmable brick, which connects via Bluetooth to a tablet (iOS/Android). It also come with a vision sensor (Color+Distance sensor), so you can interact with your creation. With BOOST, you can make your LEGO creations come to life.
